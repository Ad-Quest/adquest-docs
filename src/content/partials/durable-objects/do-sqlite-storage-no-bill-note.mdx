:::note[Storage billing on SQLite-backed Durable Objects]
Storage billing for SQLite-backed Durable Objects will be enabled in January 2026, with a target date of January 7, 2026 (no earlier). Only SQLite storage usage on and after the billing target date will incur charges. For more information, refer to [Billing for SQLite Storage](/changelog/2026-01-07-durable-objects-sqlite-storage-billing/).
:::
